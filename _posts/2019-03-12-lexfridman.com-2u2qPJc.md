### Leslie Kaelbling: Reinforcement Learning, Planning, and Robotics | MIT | Artificial Intelligence Podcast

Leslie Kaelbling is a roboticist and professor at MIT. She is recognized for her work in reinforcement learning, planning, robot navigation, and several other topics in AI. She won the IJCAI Computers and Thought Award and was the editor-in-chief of the prestigious Journal of Machine Learning Research. Video version is available on YouTube.
