### Levels - Headspace combined with Fitbit | Product Hunt

Levels is a smart meditation cushion and app. The cushion uses innovative sensors and digital filtering algorithms to measure someone’s stillness, heart rate, and breathing patterns just by sitting on it. With the biofeedback information provided by the cushion, meditators can easily understand the benefits of meditation + see how it affects them.
