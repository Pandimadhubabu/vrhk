### smileML - User research powered by emotion recognition | Product Hunt

smileML is a user research software that analyzes user emotion in interview videos, transcribes speech, and allows you to share insight with collaborative tools. With a visual timeline, you can see where the moments of delight or frustration are and jump right to them.
