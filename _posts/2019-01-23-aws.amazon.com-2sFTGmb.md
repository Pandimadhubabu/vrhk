### Extract and visualize clinical entities using Amazon Comprehend Medical | Amazon Web Services

Amazon Comprehend Medical is a new HIPAA-eligible service that uses machine learning (ML) to extract medical information with high accuracy. This reduces the cost, time, and effort of processing large amounts of unstructured medical text.
