### Fireflies Chrome Extension - Transcribe meetings from Google Calendar | Product Hunt

With 1 click, you can invite Fireflies while scheduling meetings on Google Calendar. If the meeting has a web-conferencing link or dial-in, our AI assistant, Fred will join the call to capture &amp; automatically transcribe it for you.
